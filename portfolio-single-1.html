Graduation Date June 1st, 2016
Stream Bachelor's in Computer Science Engineering
University Anna University
College Panimalar Institute of Technology

When I was a kid, I dreamed of becoming a marine engineer and building ships that could explore the depths of the ocean. But as I got older, I realized that maybe spending all that time at sea wasn't the best idea for someone who cant stay away from family for a long time from looking at a picture of a boat at home.

As it turns out, being a software developer is a lot like being a shipbuilder, just with less danger of getting seasick. I may not be exploring the ocean depths, but I am exploring the depths of programming and building new and exciting things every day.

So, even though I never became a marine engineer, I'm still navigating new waters in the tech world. And who knows, maybe one day I'll build a program that can explore the ocean for me, so I can stay safely on land with my feet firmly planted.
