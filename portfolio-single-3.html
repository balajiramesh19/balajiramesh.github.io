Training Days @ Infosys,Mysore

Training Days

When I first started my training at Infosys Mysore, I was excited to learn Python and Java. During my generic training in Python, I thought I had the language mastered until one day, I accidentally wrote a program that kept repeating itself infinitely. I didn't realize my mistake until my computer began to emit smoke and eventually caught fire! Fortunately, the fire was quickly put out and nobody was hurt, but I learned a valuable lesson about the importance of properly testing and debugging code.

As for my stream training in Java, I was initially intimidated by the language's complexity and all of its various libraries. One day, my instructor gave us an assignment to create a program that would simulate a self-driving car. I was so focused on making the car's navigation work properly that I completely forgot to program the brakes! The car ended up crashing into a virtual tree, and we all had a good laugh about it in the end.


Despite these mishaps, I learned a lot during my training at Infosys. I discovered that programming can be both challenging and fun, and that the most important thing is to never give up in the face of obstacles. Plus, I made some great friends along the way who also had their own amusing programming mishaps to share.
