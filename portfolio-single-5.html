First Product Company

Division Risk and Disputes Platform
Location Chennai
Role Software Development Engineer II

When I decided to leave JPMC and join Paypal, I knew it was going to be a big change. Not only was I moving from one company to another, but I was also transitioning from finance to product. And to top it all off, it was the middle of a pandemic!
On my first day at Paypal, I was excited to start my new job but also a bit nervous about what to expect. I logged into my computer and was greeted by a welcome email from HR, complete with a list of all the benefits and perks I would be receiving. I couldn't help but laugh when I saw "free snacks" listed as one of the benefits - after all, in the middle of a pandemic, who doesn't need a good snack to get through the day?

As the weeks went on, I started to get the hang of things at Paypal. I enjoyed working on product and being part of a company that was so focused on innovation. But as much as I loved my new job, there were definitely some challenges to working from home during a pandemic. For one, my coworkers and I had to get creative with our communication - we used video chats, Slack messages, and even virtual happy hours to stay connected. And then there were the distractions - I couldn't tell you how many times I had to mute myself on a call because my dog was barking in the background!

My Work @ Paypal

Worked as a backend developer on building the risk and disputes platform. Worked on the three business-critical programs - dispute resolution fee, crypto launch on disputes program, and making the components geo-aware. Automated the manual intervention across the platform.
