Days at Microsoft

Leaving Paypal was a tough decision, but when I got the opportunity to work at Microsoft - my dream company - I knew I couldn't pass it up. Plus, the fact that I would be working on the payments platform, something I was passionate about, made the decision even easier.

My first few weeks at Microsoft were a whirlwind. I was excited to be working for a company that had been on my career wishlist for years, and I was eager to learn everything I could about the payments platform. But what really blew me away was the culture at Microsoft. It was a company that encouraged innovation and creativity, and the people who worked there were some of the brightest minds in the industry.

Of course, I couldn't talk about Microsoft without mentioning Sathya Nadella. He was not only the CEO but also my biggest inspiration. His vision for the company and his leadership style were truly inspiring, and I was thrilled to be part of a company that he led.

As I settled into my new role, I found myself amazed at the scope of the work we were doing. The payments platform was a massive undertaking, but I was surrounded by talented engineers and leaders who made it feel manageable. I was able to learn and grow in ways I never thought possible, and every day I felt grateful to be part of such an incredible company.

Looking back on my time at Microsoft, I can honestly say it was one of the most rewarding experiences of my career. The work was challenging but fulfilling, and the people I worked with were some of the best I've ever met. I'm grateful for the opportunity to have worked at such an amazing company, and I know I'll always look back on my time there with fondness.
